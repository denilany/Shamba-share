Our Mission

Connecting landowners with farmers to create sustainable agricultural partnerships that benefit communities and the environment.

Our Story

Shamba Share was born from a simple observation: while many landowners have fertile, unused land, numerous passionate farmers lack access to affordable farming space. We built this platform to bridge this gap and create meaningful connections in the agricultural community.

Since our founding in 2020, we've helped facilitate hundreds of successful land-sharing partnerships, contributing to local food production and sustainable farming practices across the country.
